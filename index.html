How it works

A match finder for musicians

Ever wanted to form a music band but found it too hard to find fellow band members? Here's a solution to it! A Tinder for musicians.

Artists can swipe through a list of others and send requests to the ones they find impressive for their band.

It'll also have a feature that will find gigs for the bands, which would mainly benefit those who are recently starting in this field.
